Kitchen.co - Gravity Forms Add-on

== Description ==

Kitchen.co add-on for Gravity Forms automatically creates project conversations in your Kitchen workspace after Ð° Gravity Form submissions on your website.

== Setup ==

- Go to Apps screen in your Kitchen Workspace and enable the "Gravity Forms" integration
- Follow the provided setup steps

== Features ==

- Seamless integration from your marketing site to your client communication platform
- Choose which gravity forms on your website to create project conversation in Kitchen
- Map gravity forms fields to client email and name in Kitchen
- See the complete Gravity Forms subscription in your Kitchen Workspace
